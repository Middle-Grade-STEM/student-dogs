What are the types of dogs?

There are many different types of dogs. Some are just companians that are your friends. Another type of dogs are guide dogs that help people who are blind to navigate around obstacles. This helps blind people have better confidence in doing day to day activities. Mobility dogs on the other hand help people with physical tasks such as opening/closing doors, turning lights on and off, and getting something for their owner. Search and rescue dogs help find people lost or trapped somewhere whether in a mountain or in some debris after a natural disaster.
